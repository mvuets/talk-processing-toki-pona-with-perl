ma Mewika li pona anu ma Ilan li pona?
///"by Gustaf Mjolnir-Cohen » Thu Feb 15, 2007 10:06 am"


jan lawa pi ma Mewika li utala toki e jan lawa ma Ilan.

jan lawa pi ma Mewika li toki e ni: 'jan Mewika li wile pali e ijo la,
ona li ken pali e ijo.  taso la, jan Ilan ken ala'.

jan Mewika li ken pali e ni: jan li toki e ni: 'jan lawa pi ma Mewike li ike mute!'

jan lawa pi ma Ilan li toki e ni: 'a! ma Ilan li sama.  kin la jan Ilan
mute li ken toki e ni: jan lawa pi ma Mewike li ike mute!'
